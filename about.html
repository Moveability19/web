About Us

Unlock your child's potential with care that feels both precise and warm.

MovEability combines pediatric clinical expertise with a play-based environment so children feel encouraged and parents feel supported at every stage.

Our approach is built around meaningful movement.

We prioritize long-term function, independence, and confidence by combining assessment, guided therapy, parent education, and joyful repetition.
